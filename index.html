About US

Dr. Richard Fernandes, Ph.D, MBA - Chief Executive Officer and Co-Founder

With a first class honours BSc degree (Science), a Ph.D (Biochemistry) and an honours MBA, Luxcel co-founder and CEO, Dr Richard S.Fernandes, has spent his entire career in the Life sciences.

Dr Fernandes has extensive within FDA approved pharmaceutical faciliries (Schering Plough, Corc; Leo Laboratories, Dublin, Ireland),in areas such an Analytical Method Development, Validation, Technology Transfer and in implementing cGMP and cGLP working practices and system.

Prior to his work in industry, Dr Fernandes held post-doctoral research and lecturing positions in University College Cork. As a post-graduate student in the lab of Prof Tom Cotter Dr Fernandes specialized in the field of Apoprosis and published in a number of preer-reviewed journals.

Professor Dmitri B. Papkovsky - Chief Scientific Officer and Co-Fouder

Prof Papkovsky has long been involved in the research on the phosphorescent porphyrin probes, optical oxygen sensing, fluorescence spectroscopy and their biognostic and screening applications.

He has 80+ journal papers and books, 8 patents. He is also in charge of the Biophysics and Bioanalysis research lab at Biochemistry Department, University College Cork.

Prof Papkovsky is the inventor of Luxcel RST and Label Technologe.
